I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Mohammed VI Polytechnic University (UM6P), Ben Guerir, Morocco, where I have been working on coded distributed computing and high-performance computing since February 2026.

Prior to this, I held a Swiss Government Excellence Postdoctoral Fellowship at the University of St. Gallen, Switzerland (2024–2025), where I worked on coding theory and post-quantum cryptography under the supervision of Prof. Anna-Lena Horlemann and Prof. Joachim Rosenthal.

I completed my PhD in Mathematics in 2023 at Moulay Ismail University (ENSAM, Meknes, Morocco), under the supervision of Prof. Mustapha Najmeddine and in collaboration with Prof. Nuh Aydin. My doctoral research focused on the algebraic structure of linear codes invariant under linear and pseudo-linear transformations.

Research Interests

Algebra & Finite Fields and rings. Classification of semifields; isotopies between semifields; factorization of polynomials and skew polynomials over finite fields (rings); applications to coding theory and cryptography.
